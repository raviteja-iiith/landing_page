FAQs

Common questions answered

How do I get started with NxtStepEdu?
+
Getting started is easy — book a personalised demo with our team and we will set up your school, import your existing data, and have you live on the platform within a day. Pricing plans are tailored to your school size; contact us for a custom quote.
How does the Razorpay fee payment work?
+
Parents receive payment requests inside the app. They can pay via UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, or Razorpay wallet. Payments reflect instantly in your admin dashboard; automated receipts are sent to parents; settlements happen T+2 days to your school's bank account.
Can we migrate data from our existing system?
+
Absolutely. NxtStepEdu supports migration from Excel, Google Sheets, and most common school ERP software. Our onboarding team handles migration at no extra cost and verifies data accuracy before you go live.
Is student data stored securely?
+
Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We host on AWS Mumbai data centres, ensuring data residency in India. We comply with DPDPA guidelines and never share data with third parties.
Does it work on mobile phones?
+
Yes. NxtStepEdu is fully responsive and runs on any modern browser. We also offer native iOS and Android apps optimised for low-bandwidth conditions — accessible across urban and rural India alike.
