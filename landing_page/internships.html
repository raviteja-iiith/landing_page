Knowledge Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NxtStepEdu a registered company?
+

NxtStepEdu is currently run as a small, independent education initiative. We're transparent that we're not a large corporation — full registration and legal entity details, once finalized, will be published here and in our footer.

What is the refund policy?
+

Refunds are available within the first 7 days of a batch starting, before live sessions or dataset access begin. Email us and we'll process it within 5–7 business days.

Is the certificate recognized by companies?
+

It's a completion certificate, not an accredited degree or formal placement guarantee. It documents the track and project you completed — most interviewers care more about the project itself than the certificate.

Can I do this alongside college or semester exams?
+

Yes — the Self-Paced and Live Mentor tracks are designed around 4–6 hours a week, so most students manage this alongside a regular semester.

Who are the mentors, exactly?
+

Mentors are working engineers we've personally vetted. We share full names, LinkedIn profiles, and current employers with applicants once they're shortlisted, so you can verify their background yourself before paying.
